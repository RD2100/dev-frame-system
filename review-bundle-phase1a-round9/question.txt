## 变更摘要

Phase 1B: Derivation Helper and Projection Proof

在 Phase 1A 基础上新增 `derive_projection()` 函数，从 packet 数据推导 projection 字段，不发明权威。

### 新增功能

`derive_projection(payload) -> dict`:
- computed_status: completed / blocked / insufficient_evidence / reviewing / ready
- blocked_reason: 从决策的 payload.blocked_reasons 或默认值推导
- evidence_summary: total/supporting/rejecting/inconclusive 计数
- decision_summary: review_outcome, gate_outcome, latest_decision_id
- allowed_actions: 按 computed_status 映射（如 blocked → [view, escalate]）
