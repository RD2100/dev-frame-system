Smith & Chen (2023) propose a context-aware retrieval framework that combines metadata filtering with keyword-based scoring for academic writing systems. Key contribution is the weighted multi-signal approach: metadata relevance (chapter match, tag overlap) weighted at 0.4, keyword match at 0.6.

Relevance to current work: This is the theoretical basis for our A3 retrieval pipeline's scoring formula. The paper validates the approach on a corpus of 500 literature notes.
