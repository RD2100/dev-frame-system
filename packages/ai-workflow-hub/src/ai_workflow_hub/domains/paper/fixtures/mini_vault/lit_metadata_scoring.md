Lee (2021) develops a metadata-driven relevance scoring system for systematic literature reviews. The system uses chapter alignment, tag overlap, and publication recency as scoring signals.

Relevance: Validates our metadata_filter.py approach. Lee's system achieves 87% agreement with expert judgments on a test set of 200 sources.
